History and Company Information

TANTAWAN CONSTRUCTION AND SERVICE CO., LTD (TANTAWAN) heads a group of companies established in 2004 providing the service of :

  • Manpower supply for engineers, supervisors and skilled personnel.
  • Shop pre-fabrication and Site construction.
  • Maintenance, Modification, Revamping and Upgrading of Facilities.
  • Tool & Equipment Rental Services.

TCS is currently able to provide One-Stop End to End Solution for EPC Green Field Developments and Brown
Field Modification Works both in Upstream and Downstream Oil & Gas Industries.
